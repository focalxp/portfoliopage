Automatic
Greenpass

Automatically fills out a COVID-19 Health Check form and sends the result through email

Our school requires students to fill out a precautionary COVID-19 health form in the morning and show them the completion screen of the survery. After minimal sleep from homeworking and studying for tests, the last thing students want is to wake up from their nap in the car or in the bus to fill out a repetitive form. My solution was to create a program that not only fills out the form but sends the user an email of the completed page so that the school could verify their submission.

The program first gets access to the email of the bot that sends the emails. Then, the program accesses the stored information of all the users such as name, grade, ID, and email. It traverses this information and fills out the correct contents for each person. When the content is submitted the program screenshots the result and sends it to its designated user.
